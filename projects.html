Currently Developing a Visual Novel!

I'm using the python visual novel engine, Ren'Py to make a kinetic visual novel. I plan to make this the definitive version of my discontinued Webcomic which you can find on Webtoon!

This story follows an amnesiac lynx named Lunar and an inadequate water champion, Aqua. Together with Aqua's determination and Lunar's talent for cooking, they travel the world to reconnect relations with the elemental tribes and save their people from impending natural disasters.
